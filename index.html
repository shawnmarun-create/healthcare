Our story

Redefining healthcare, one patient at a time.

Founded in 2022, Alian was born from a simple belief: healthcare should be accessible, warm, and human. Too many patients face endless wait times, confusing bills, and rushed visits. We set out to change that.

Today, Alian operates a hybrid model — state-of-the-art clinics and 24/7 virtual care — serving over 50,000 members nationwide. Our team includes top physicians, therapists, and nurses who prioritize listening over rushing.

We are proud to be a certified B-Corp, committed to equitable care for all communities. Every decision we make puts patients first.
